Import list, Export ABC tunes

My tunes lets you import a simple text list of your tunes; then it can match them to tunes from its database, and give you the full ABC music.

Editable ABC and score

MyTunes displays the music score of your tunes, but all tues are fully editable; meanwhile, all the abc metadata are preserved.

Play your tune at your speed

How's that Rakish Paddy, I'm sure I know it, but...
Use MyTunes as a fast reminder, or use it to exercise, slowing or speeding the music as you like

Tunes by date

You can attach a learning date to a tune, and you can group them by month, so you'll keep your practive up to date.

The same music in your pocket and in your bag

My tunes syncs between iPhone and iPad, so your collection will be the same across your devices
